## Tips for Pakistan Market

1. **Use PKR currency** - Always mention PKR or Pakistani Rupees
2. **Mention Pakistan** - Include "Pakistan" in headlines when possible
3. **PayPal emphasis** - PayPal is trusted in Pakistan, emphasize it
4. **Mobile-friendly** - Many users access via mobile, ensure ads are clear
5. **Local language mix** - Consider mixing English with Urdu keywords
6. **Trust signals** - Use social proof (2M+ users, ratings)
7. **Specific amounts** - Use exact amounts in PKR (Rs 433, Rs 1,568, etc.)
8. **Time-sensitive** - Pakistan market responds well to urgency

## A/B Testing Recommendations

Test these variations:
- **Amounts:** "Rs 433" vs "up to Rs 433" vs "Rs 433 per survey"
- **Currency:** "PKR" vs "Rs" vs "Pakistani Rupees"
- **Location:** "in Pakistan" vs "Pakistan" vs "for Pakistanis"
- **Action:** "Join" vs "Start" vs "Earn"
- **Proof:** "2M+ users" vs "thousands" vs "millions"
